Our story

Established in December 2012, Blueprint Lounge has managed to capture the hearts of Ghanaians as well as visitors in and out of Accra. With one mission in mind; there's a much comfort at Blueprint Lounge!An open air bar famous for it's signature lights, cocktails, music performances and unpretentious ambience.
